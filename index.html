Hi, I'm Varun!

Architectural Designer | Model Maker | Transdisciplinary Innovator

To me, being an architect is not simply about your ability to design buildings. It's about having an acute spatial awareness, being able to visualise in three-dimensions and to choreograph human movement through physical structures and space. It's about understanding who we are designing for, how things will be used, and most importantly, why.

I studied a Bachelor of Design in Architecture and Bachelor of Creative Intelligence and Innovation at the University of Technology, Sydney. I have experience using laser cutters, CNC machines, woodworking machinery, 3D modelling software, and the Adobe Creative Suite.
