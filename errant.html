UI Consulting and Newsletter Issue Art

Nikhil Mathew & Varun Mathew

Errant-Labs + The Prolix Post, 2017

I was approached by my brother to help redesign the user interface of his website and start creating artworks for his literary newsletter. This was an opportunity for me to start getting real experience with a public facing website and trying to come up with a clean, unique design aesthetic.

Further this was an opportunity for me to start creating art and illustrations which has been something that I've wanted to do for a while now. I purchased a drawing tablet at the beginning of the year and have been able to make great use of it with the little free time I had during my hectic final year of my undergraduate degree.

My user interface learnings and understandings that has shaped the design of his website include:

  • Colour palettes and colour design

  • Translating typographical concepts from print to web

  • Stylistic design decisions that reflect the underlying goal of his work
